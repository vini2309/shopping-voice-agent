What it helps with

A simple shopping assistant for common in-store questions.

01

Find products

Ask where something is and hear the aisle, bay, and stock status in a short answer.

02

Compare options

Ask for the best item in a category and get a recommendation based on catalog signals.

03

Handle store questions

Ask about returns, pickup, substitutions, accessibility, or what to do when a shelf is empty.

Try saying

Natural questions, short spoken answers.

“Where are paper towels?” “What dog food do you recommend?” “Can I return opened electronics?” “The shelf is empty but the app says it is in stock.”

Voice qualityTests whether accents still preserve the shopper’s intent
GroundingChecks that answers stay supported by available store evidence
