# Price Match and Promotions

## In-Store Price Checks

If a shelf tag and register price disagree, the associate should verify the item, shelf location, and tag date. The customer should be routed to a register lead or service desk for any adjustment.

## Competitor Price Match

This synthetic store does not guarantee competitor price matching from other retailers. Associates should not promise a competitor match; they should send the customer to the service desk for current promotional rules.

## App Price Difference

If the app shows a different price, ask the customer to confirm the store location in the app. App prices can differ by fulfillment method, delivery, pickup, and local store availability.
