# Returns and Exchanges

## Standard Return Window

Most unopened general merchandise can be returned within 90 days with a receipt. The associate should direct customers to the service desk for return processing and should not promise a refund before the item and receipt are checked.

## Opened or Used Items

Opened consumables, damaged products, and used personal-care items require service-desk review. The associate can explain that the service desk will check the receipt, item condition, and return eligibility.

## Electronics Exception

Electronics and high-value devices may have a shorter return window and may require accessories, serial-number verification, and the original packaging. If the customer asks about gaming consoles, controllers, laptops, phones, or tablets, direct them to the service desk for the exact policy review.
