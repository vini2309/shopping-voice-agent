# Online Pickup and Curbside

## Pickup Location

Online grocery pickup orders are staged near the front pickup doors or curbside pickup bays. Customers should check in through the app or use the posted pickup phone number when they arrive.

## Order Not Ready

If an order is not ready, the associate should ask the customer to check the app status first. If the app says ready but the order is missing, route the customer to pickup support or the service desk.

## Substitutions

For online grocery substitutions, customers can accept or reject substitutions in the app before pickup. Associates should not guarantee an exact replacement unless the substitution is already listed on the order.
