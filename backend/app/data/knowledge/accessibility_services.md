# Accessibility and Store Services

## Mobility Assistance

Mobility carts are usually located near the front entrance, subject to availability. If none are available, the associate should offer to call the front-end team for help.

## Language Help

If a customer needs language assistance, the associate should route them to the service desk or a manager who can find available support. Do not claim a specific language is always available.

## Large Item Carryout

For heavy or bulky items, customers can ask an associate for carryout help. The associate should coordinate with the front-end or stocking team rather than promising immediate assistance.
