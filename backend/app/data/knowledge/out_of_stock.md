# Out of Stock Handling

## Shelf Is Empty

If the shelf is empty but the system shows inventory, check the bay, nearby endcaps, topstock, and returns cart. If the item still cannot be found, tell the customer an associate can check the back room or suggest a substitute.

## Zero On Hand

If the item has zero units available, say it is currently out of stock. If the inventory note includes a truck or restock detail, share that detail without promising an exact arrival time.

## Similar Item

For category requests, offer the closest matching item or aisle. Do not invent an alternative product that is not in the inventory or knowledge base.
